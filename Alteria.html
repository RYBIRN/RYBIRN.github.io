Alteria

Alteria is my current video game hobby project that I have been developing using Unreal Engine for the last 4 months. Currently I am working through the weapon, and damage system. The game currently contains 21 different guns each with their own unique stats, and attributes.

I have setup a line trace debug system to show the velocity of the bullet as it travels:

  • Red shows the bullet is greater than 75% of its max velocity
  • Yellow is greater than 50%
  • Green is greater than 25%
  • blue is below 25
  • and white is under 10%

The bullet experiences drag force and bullet drop as it moves through the air.

Alteria Features a penetration and riccochet system that takes into account the material type, thickness of the object, angle of impact, and velocity of the bullet. The bullet can penetrate and riccochet off any number of objects until it loses all of it's energy.
